Shipping, Returns & More
Shipping & Returns

Orders are processed within 2 business days. Within the Netherlands delivery takes 3 to 7 business days; international orders 7 to 21 business days. Shipping is calculated at checkout and is free on EU orders over €150.

You may return your shirt within 14 days of receiving it, unworn and unwashed with any tags attached. Email us first at info@shirtsociete.com so we can confirm the return address. See our Refund Policy and Shipping Policy.

Authenticity Guarantee

Every shirt in our collection is checked by hand before it is listed. We verify the manufacturer tags, stitching, badge application and wash label against the year and edition it claims to be.

We only sell originals. If a piece is a reissue or a replica, that is stated clearly in the description. Any flaw, repair or sign of wear is photographed and mentioned.

What is Vintage Fußballbörse?
i

Vintage Fußballbörse is Germany's most established vintage football fair, held multiple times a year across major cities. Each edition brings together over 1,000 collectors from across Europe — from casual fans to serious collectors and dealers. The fairs are a unique meeting place where rare match worn shirts, vintage originals and club memorabilia change hands and stories are shared. Shirt Société attends as a regular exhibitor, bringing a carefully curated selection of pieces to every event. Follow the fair on Instagram: @vintagefussball

Who We Are

A Collector's Eye,
A Curator's Touch

Shirt Société was born out of a genuine passion for football and the stories behind the shirts that defined the game. What started as a personal collection quickly grew into something bigger: a curated platform for collectors around the globe.

From vintage clubs to match worn pieces worn by legends, every shirt in our collection has been hand-picked for its authenticity, rarity and history. We don't just sell shirts. We preserve football heritage.

Active at vintage football fairs across Germany, including Vintage Fußballbörse in Hamburg (04.02.2026), Cologne (31.05.2026), Düsseldorf (13.06.2026) and most recently Frankfurt (04.07.2026), Shirt Société has built a reputation among collectors for quality, knowledge and passion. Next stop: Leipzig, October 4th 2026.
