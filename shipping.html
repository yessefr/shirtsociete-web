Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At Shirt Société we want your vintage pieces to arrive in perfect condition and on time. Here is how we handle and ship your orders.

Processing time

Orders are processed within 2 business days. Because nearly every shirt is one of a kind, it is carefully checked and packed before dispatch. You will receive an email once your order has shipped, with tracking information where available.

Shipping within the Netherlands

We offer standard shipping within the Netherlands. Estimated delivery is 3 to 7 business days, depending on your location.

International shipping

We ship to selected countries. Fees and delivery times vary by destination, and estimated delivery is 7 to 21 business days. International customers are responsible for any customs fees, taxes or duties charged by their own country.

Shipping costs

Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on your location and the weight of the items.

Order tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number where available, so you can follow your shipment on the carrier's website.

Lost or damaged packages

If your package arrives damaged, contact us immediately at info@shirtsociete.com with photos of the damage, so we can help you with a replacement or refund. For packages lost in transit we will work with you and the carrier to resolve it.

Returns & exchanges

Please see our Refund Policy for information on returns and exchanges.

Any questions about your shipment? Email us at info@shirtsociete.com. Thank you for shopping with Shirt Société.
