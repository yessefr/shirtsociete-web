Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

We have a 14-day return policy, which means you have 14 days after receiving your item to request a return.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unworn and unwashed, with any tags attached and in its original packaging. You will also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

To start a return, contact us at info@shirtsociete.com. Items sent back without first requesting a return will not be accepted. Please email us first, as items may be stored off-site and we will confirm the correct return address for your order.

Damages and issues

Please inspect your order on arrival and contact us immediately if the item is defective, damaged, or if you received the wrong item, so we can evaluate the issue and make it right.

Full disclosure & sizing

We take pride in describing every item accurately. Any flaws or imperfections are highlighted in the listing, and the size is specified in the title and description. Because these are vintage pieces, small signs of age and wear are part of their character. If an item fits differently from its stated size, we note this in the description.

Exceptions

Unfortunately we cannot accept returns on gift cards. Sale items may have specific conditions noted at the time of purchase.

Exchanges

The fastest way to get what you want is to return the item you have, and once the return is accepted, make a separate purchase for the new item.

EU 14-day cooling-off period

If your order is shipped within the European Union, you have the right to cancel or return your order within 14 days, for any reason and without justification. Your item must be returned in the same condition you received it, unworn and unwashed, with tags and original packaging, along with proof of purchase.

Return costs

In most cases you are responsible for the cost of return postage, unless the item was faulty or not as described. We recommend obtaining proof of return shipment from your postal service, as any damage during return is your responsibility until it reaches us.

Refunds

We will notify you once we have received and inspected your return and let you know if the refund is approved. If approved, you will be refunded to your original payment method within 10 business days. Please note it can take additional time for your bank or card provider to process and post the refund. If more than 15 business days have passed since we approved your return, contact us at info@shirtsociete.com.
