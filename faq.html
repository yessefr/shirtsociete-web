Authenticity
How do I know a shirt is authentic?

Every shirt is checked by hand before it is listed. We compare the manufacturer tags, stitching, badge application, sponsor print and wash label against the year and edition the shirt claims to be. Details like the tag style and the way a crest is attached changed almost every season, which makes them reliable markers.

We only sell originals. If a piece is a later reissue rather than an original of its era, that is stated clearly in the description.

What is the difference between match worn and match issued?

A match worn shirt was actually worn by the player during the game. A match issued shirt was prepared for that specific player for a particular match, but was not worn on the pitch.

Keep in mind that a match worn shirt may have been washed after the game, so it does not always carry visible traces of play. Whenever a shirt is worn or issued, we state which one it is in the listing.

Are the shirts in good condition?

Each listing states the condition, and any flaw, repair, fading or loose stitching is photographed and mentioned in the description. These shirts are decades old, so small imperfections are normal and part of their character.

If something is not visible in the photos, it is not there. When in doubt, message us and we will take extra pictures.

Ordering & Shipping
Is there only one of each shirt?

Yes. Almost every piece in the collection is one of one. Once a shirt is sold, that exact shirt is gone. This is why pieces disappear quickly after a drop, and why we recommend saving the ones you like.

How long does delivery take?

Orders are processed within 2 business days. Within the Netherlands delivery takes 3 to 7 business days. International orders take 7 to 21 business days depending on the destination.

Shipping is calculated at checkout based on your country. See our Shipping Policy for the details.

Do you ship outside the EU?

Yes, we ship to selected countries worldwide. Please note that customs fees, import taxes or duties charged by your own country are your responsibility, and are not included in the price you pay at checkout.

Which payment methods can I use?

Payments are handled securely through Mollie, which supports iDEAL, Bancontact, credit and debit cards, PayPal and several other methods depending on your country. We never see or store your card or bank details.

Returns
Can I return a shirt?

Yes. You have 14 days from receiving your order to request a return, no reason needed. The shirt must come back in the same condition you received it, unworn and unwashed, with any tags still attached.

Email us first at info@shirtsociete.com so we can confirm the return address. Full details are in our Refund Policy.

Who pays for return shipping?

Return postage is at your own cost, unless the shirt arrived damaged or was not as described. In that case we cover it. We recommend sending returns with tracking, since the item is your responsibility until it reaches us.
